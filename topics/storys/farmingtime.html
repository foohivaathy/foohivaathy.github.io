Farming time

I keep running out of time a lot. I always try to find some wherever I can, but it's very good at hiding in places like work and the web. I thought I could find some time on the web, but sometimes I didn't have time to find time.

The web is built by spiders.

There were times where I tried to make time, but I couldn't find time to get the ingredients. Looking on the web for ingredients was leading to more and more lost time.

It evaporates quickly like pouring alcohol on a hot pan. Alcohol leads to a lot of lost time too, but some people enjoy losing time like that. I had a beer the other day. It didn't taste that great and my head spun. Beer is stupid.

I then tried to grow some time, but that involved using more time... which I couldn't find much of in the first place. Growing time is harder than growing a bonsai. You can only prune what you can do, but what you can do never comes back.

Time is a plant that grows backwards.

I've been told I'll like beer if I drink more of it, but I don't have time for that.

Today, I thought about Farming time - Sunday, July 14, 2013
